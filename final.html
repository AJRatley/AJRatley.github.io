Mini Me

I made this create your own avatar game called "mini me". I made it as my final project for Digital Media, a class I took in San Jose. I drew over 250 assets to create diversity in this game, and although it isn't perfect it is my first major p5.js game design project so I am proud of it due to the potential. To narrow down the assets drawing process I only made this suitable for creating feminine presenting people, but if I were to expand the project in future I would create more avatar options. I drew the assets in Adobe Illustrator and used W3schools to teach me the coding I didn't know.
The outcome isn't perfect because certain assets don't sit where they should due to one coordinate being used for all the noses etc. Also, I have noticed that you need to do the hair last or redo it at the end as the eyebrows change colour each time you select a colour for a different asset.
Please try out the game below, press play, and once you are happy with the outcome press on your avatar for the option to save a download or restart.

Please see my code below.
